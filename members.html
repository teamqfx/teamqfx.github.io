Team QFX

Team QFX is a UK based Vertical Formation Skydiving (VFS) team, established in 2011. The team won Silver in the 2012 and 2013 UK skydiving Nationals, and competed in the world championships in Dubai in 2012.

All 5 team members were participants in the new 30 way 2013 UK Head Down record, and most team members also hold current European and World records.

The team achieved a major increase in average score at the UK Nationals; from 5.4 in 2012, to 8.9 in 2013. this is attributed to increased team experience, a few lineup changes and a dedicated training plan.

The team plans to keep the same lineup for 2014 and beyond,but greatly increase the amount of training, and expects to continue going from strength to strength.
